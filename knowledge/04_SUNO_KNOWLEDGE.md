# SUNO Knowledge

This file is the music and mix craft reference for the MAMILAS SUNO agent.

## Core Job

The SUNO agent does not design scenes. It carries the emotion and rhythm of the
edit. The highest success is music that is felt without overpowering the message.

## Voice Pocket

MAMILAS work often contains voice-over or text-led explanation. Music should:

- leave the midrange open
- avoid pushing lead melody above speech
- keep kick/bass from swallowing dialogue
- make transitions serve edit points
- use silence and room tone when needed

## Deriving Music From World

Render World gives only sonic color:

- Real commercial: clean premium pulse, restrained expensive feel, room for
  product SFX.
- Documentary: minimal acoustic texture, room tone, honesty, non-manipulative
  emotion.
- Social real: short hook, native energy, fast but clean.
- Education tactile: pluck, mallet, soft strings, small mechanism sounds.
- Arcane/painterly: low strings/brass pressure, dark but controlled release.
- Anime/comic: impact accent and rhythmic cut energy without crushing VO.
- Ghibli/nature: breath, piano/woodwind, organic calm.
- Stop-motion: music box, muted pluck, handmade percussion.

## Material Sound Design

Material is a small sound-detail layer:

- paper: fold, rustle, page lift
- clay: soft thud, wobble
- wood: click, gear, tap
- glass/lightbox: shimmer, lamp hum
- fabric: brush, stitch, muffled motion

These details must not take over the main music idea.

## Suno Prompt Contents

A strong Suno prompt includes:

- mood + function
- instrumentation
- tempo feel
- scene arc
- VO pocket
- mix restraint
- ending behavior

## Negative

Prevent:

- vocals unless requested
- artist imitation
- copyrighted melody/lyrics
- trailer bombast
- overcompressed harsh mix
- busy lead under narration

## Repair

Bad: "epic cinematic emotional music".  
Good: "restrained warm pulse, muted piano motif, soft brushed percussion, low pad
only under proof beats, no vocals, leave midrange clear for Turkish narration,
small lift into final hold."
